Welcome to our comprehensive course on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics! This course is designed to take you from fundamental concepts to advanced implementations in the exciting field of intelligent humanoid robotics.

## What You'll Learn

Throughout this course, you'll explore four key modules:

1. **ROS 2 Foundations**: The communication backbone of robotic systems
2. **Digital Twins**: Physics-based simulation for robot development
3. **NVIDIA Isaac**: AI-powered perception and navigation
4. **Vision-Language-Action**: Complete autonomous humanoid systems

## Target Audience

This course is designed for:
- AI and robotics students
- Engineers interested in humanoid robotics
- Researchers working on embodied AI
- Developers building robotic applications

## Getting Started

We recommend starting with Module 1 to establish the foundational concepts that will be built upon throughout the course. Each module builds on the previous one, creating a comprehensive understanding of the humanoid robotics ecosystem.

Stay tuned for more updates and content as we continue to expand this course!
