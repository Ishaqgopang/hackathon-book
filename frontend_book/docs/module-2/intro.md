# Module 2: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)

## Overview

Welcome to Module 2 of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics course. In this module, you will learn about digital twins and simulation environments that accelerate the development of humanoid robots. You'll explore physics-based simulation using Gazebo and high-fidelity visualization with Unity.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:
- Understand the concept and importance of digital twins in robotics
- Create physics-based simulations using Gazebo
- Design environments and worlds for robot testing
- Understand Unity's role in high-fidelity visualization
- Work with simulated sensors for perception pipeline development
